# Operational Objectives

## Strategic Goals

1. **Efficiency & Productivity**
   - Machine utilization >90%
   - Multi-shift continuous operations

2. **Quality Leadership**
   - ISO 9001:2015 certified
   - World-class quality standards

3. **Flexibility & Responsiveness**
   - Adapt to market demands
   - Custom and export orders

4. **Cost Competitiveness**
   - Large-scale production efficiency
   - Strategic sourcing

5. **Sustainability**
   - Environmental responsibility
   - Employee development
