# Future Challenges (2025-2030)

## Key Challenges

1. **Material Cost Volatility**
   - Dependence on imported polymers
   - Global oil & resin price fluctuations
   - Need to expand recycling capacity

2. **Stricter Regulations**
   - Zero-waste circular economy by 2030
   - EPR laws in export markets (EU, North America)

3. **Infrastructure Constraints**
   - Inconsistent energy supply
   - Logistics bottlenecks
   - Need for renewable energy

4. **Technology Transition**
   - Workforce retraining for automation
   - Balancing automation with employment

5. **Global Market Dynamics**
   - Export uncertainty & competition
   - Currency volatility & tariffs
