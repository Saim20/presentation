# Continuous Improvement

## TPM & Kaizen Culture

- **Total Productive Maintenance:** Zero breakdowns, defects, accidents
- **Kaizen workshops:** Employee-driven improvements
- **Autonomous Maintenance:** Operator ownership
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Condition monitoring

## Lean & Six Sigma

- **5S organization** on factory floors
- **Six Sigma tools** for problem-solving
- **ABC inventory analysis**
- Root-cause analysis & Pareto charts

## Impact

- **50% output increase** on certain machines
- Millions in annual savings from waste reduction
- Improved product quality & consistency
- Enhanced employee engagement
