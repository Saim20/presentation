# The 4Vs of Operations Management

## VOLUME - High Volume Production
- **20,000+ tons/month** production capacity
- **620+ molding machines** operating continuously
- Economies of scale advantage
- Low unit costs, high efficiency
- Growth trajectory from 2003 to present

## VARIETY - Extensive Product Range
- **Thousands of SKUs** across multiple categories
- Product categories: Household, Furniture, Industrial, Pipes
- **7,500+ molds** enabling wide customization
- Flexible manufacturing capabilities
- Injection, extrusion, and blow molding technologies

## VARIATION - Demand Fluctuation Management
- Seasonal demand patterns (domestic market)
- Export vs. domestic order variations
- **Multi-shift (24/7)** operational flexibility
- Hybrid production system: continuous + batch
- Adaptable to market changes

## VISIBILITY - High Customer Interaction
- **3,000+ retail outlets** nationwide
- **1,950+ RFL Exclusive showrooms** (franchise model)
- **24/7 customer service** hotline
- Strong brand presence and reputation
- Direct customer feedback integration
- Multiple touchpoints across Bangladesh and 80+ export countries
