Scheduling & Bottleneck Management

Scheduling Approach

  • Master Production Schedule (MPS): Weekly targets
  • Batch Grouping: Same materials/machines together
  • Push System: Make-to-stock based on forecasts

Bottleneck Management

Primary Bottlenecks
  1. Mold Changing & Setup
    • SMED principles applied
    • Target: <10 minutes setup time
  2. Cycle Time: Cooling & injection constraints
  3. Post-Molding: Printing, labeling, assembly

Solutions: Track OEE, preventative maintenance, dedicated tooling
