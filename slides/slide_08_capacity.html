Capacity Management

Capacity Determination

Key Constraints
  • Machine Capacity: Tonnage & cycle time
  • Mold Availability: 7,500 molds for thousands of SKUs
Utilization
  • >90% capacity utilization in core products
  • 24/7 multi-shift operations
  • Limited slack for demand surges

Resource Allocation

  • High-Volume Core Products: Make-to-stock approach
  • Export/Custom Orders: Dedicated shifts and lines
  • Strategic separation to maintain flow efficiency
